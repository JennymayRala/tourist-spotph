10 Best Tourist Spots in the Philippines

White Beach in Boracay Island

Located in the central Visayas region, about an hour’s flight from Manila City, Boracay Island is home to what is widely regarded as the best tourist spot in the Philippines—White Beach. Stretching for 4 kilometers, White Beach boasts powdery, pure white sand that feels like walking on clouds...

Chocolate Hills in Bohol Island

The Chocolate Hills in Bohol Island, located in the central Visayas island region of the Philippines, are a true natural wonder and a must-see destination. Recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Natural Monument, these 1,268 cone-shaped hills transform into a stunning chocolate-brown hue...

Puerto Princesa Underground River in Palawan Island

Puerto Princesa City serves as the gateway to Palawan Island and is home to the world-renowned Puerto Princesa Underground River, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New 7 Wonders of the World. Spanning over 8 kilometers, it is the longest underground river in the world...

Cloud 9 surfing spot in Siargao Island

Siargao Island, located in the island region of Mindanao, south of the Philippines, has rapidly become one of the country's top destinations, especially for surf enthusiasts. Known as the country’s surfing capital and even considered by some as the surfing mecca of Asia...

Diving Spots in Cebu Island

Rizal Park in Manila City

Kayangan Lake in Coron Palawan Island

Located in the northern part of Palawan Island, the town of Coron is a coastal paradise in the Philippines known for its breathtaking natural beauty. Among its many gems, Kayangan Lake stands out as a must-visit destination. Often hailed as the cleanest lake in the Philippines...
